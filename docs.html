Navbar

Navigation bar is to navigate through web app or websites. Navbars are responsive, it changes to a hamburger menu in medium and small devices.

Supported content

Navbar comes with built-in support for handful of sub-components:

  • .navbar-toggle to toggle the hamburger in M, S, and XS screens.
  • .navbar-brand for your company or project name. It will be hidden in M, S and XS screens.
  • .navbar-brand-title It will be displayed in M, S, XS screens and hidden on L, XL screens.
  • .navbar-links for your links, that will be displayed in L, XL screens but inside hamburger menu in M, S, XS screens.
  • .search-bar to add a search bar to your navbar.
  • .nav-section to show icons with text below it. Text will be hidden in M, S, XS screens.
