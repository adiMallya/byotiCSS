Standard Avatars

The avatar component comes in 5 sizes avatar-xl (4rem), avatar-lg (3rem), avatar-std (2rem), avatar-sm (1.5rem) and avatar-xs (1.rem). 

            
               <figure class="avatar avatar-lg">
               <img src="..." alt="..."/>
               </figure>

               <figure class="avatar avatar-sm">
               <img src="..." alt="..."/>
               </figure>

Avatars with status

These avatars support status indicators. There are three online, dnd and offline, 

            
               <figure class="avatar avatar-lg">
               <img src="..." alt="..."/>
               <div class="avatar-status online">
               </figure>

               <figure class="avatar avatar-sm">
               <img src="..." alt="..."/>
               <div class="avatar-status offline">
               </figure>
