Responsive Image

Add the img-res class to the img tag. The image will grow to 100% of its parent container. 

            
                <img src="..." alt="" class="img-res" />

Round Image

Add the img-round class to the img tag to get a round image which grows to 100% of its parent container. 

            
                <img src="..." alt="" class="img-round" />
